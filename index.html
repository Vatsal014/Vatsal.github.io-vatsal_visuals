How We Work

Our process

A clear, collaborative workflow that keeps every project on track and every client informed — from the first call to the final file delivery.

01
Discovery & Brief

We deep-dive into your brand, goals, audience and budget. This shapes a creative brief that guides everything forward and ensures full alignment.

02
Concept & Pre-Production

Scripts, storyboards, moodboards, shot lists, location scouting and casting — every detail planned before a single camera rolls.

03
Production

Shoot day. Full crew, professional equipment, and focused directing — capturing exactly what the script demands with cinematic precision.

04
Post-Production & VFX

Editing, colour grading, sound design, animation and visual effects — polished to the highest standard with clear revision rounds built in.

05
Delivery & Launch

All formats for all platforms — optimised files delivered ready to publish, on time. We stay available for any tweaks post-launch.
